Our Philosophy

Elegant Revolution blends classic tailoring with modern design. We pursue a quiet kind of luxury — refined materials, expert craftsmanship and small-batch production. We make pieces that endure, both physically and stylistically.

Our process

  1. Source premium fabrics from trusted mills.
  2. Prototype and fit with our in-house cutters.
  3. Hand-finish select seams and do full quality inspection.

FAQ

Common questions about orders and shipping

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery for unworn items with tags attached. Custom and tailored items are final sale.

How long does shipping take?

Domestic (UAE) shipping typically 2–4 business days. International shipping 5–10 business days depending on destination and customs.

Do you offer tailoring?

We offer complimentary minor tailoring for a limited period after purchase. For comprehensive alterations we recommend visiting a local tailor or booking our atelier service.

Shipping & Returns

Shipping partners

We ship with trusted carriers only. Orders are dispatched from our Dubai atelier with tracking provided by email.

Returns

To initiate a return, contact hello@elrevo.com with your order number and reason. We'll send a prepaid label for domestic returns where available.
